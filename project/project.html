Innovate Time-Use

 

Hello! my dear students,here is an invaluable concept to innovate time-use. Every work of life is  in the cycle of time.  Time is priceless (any form of currency is not enough to quantify its value) though we say time is money.

1. Time brings evolution. 2. Happiness and sorrow go with time. 3. Time heals the physical and mental wounds. 4. Creation of new things both natural as well as artificial involved time, so on and so forth. In short, every aspect of life is associated with time.

Human world likes creation of new things that will benefit everybody maintaining the balance ecosystem. Let us innovate for the solution of our problems before us with proper use of time.

Time innovation is an ideation for positive approach of the proper management of time available in our life. Time income of every human being is twenty four hours a day. It is the longest time in the life of an individual in a day. Therefore, it is essential for everybody to set a programme of daily activities, with flexibility in some areas, in the convenient units of time of the day so that one can utilise the time fully.

It is obvious that Mother Nature is guiding us how to be regular and punctual in our activities. For example, the rotation and revolution of the heavenly bodies are keeping time properly. The sun rises and sets at the right time. Under right conditions, seeds germinate on time etc. etc.

Therefore, while engaging in the life activities, if we are able to follow a set time table, success is evident.

The system in the approach “Innovate time-use” is to make a time table for daily activities incorporating the following :

1. Begin your day with a prayer.

2. Love Mother Nature and follow her time table.

3. Perform at least a good useful thing dutifully in a day if possible as much as you can.

4. Allocate time according to your target.

5. Remove boredom with change of  activities.

6. Do not allocate time for the activities that will develop bad outcomes/habits.

7. Allocate sufficient time for sleep to re-energise.

8. Do exercise and avoid bad foods.

9. Think positively and take initiation at the right time.

10. Look at the problems around you and follow design thinking for the solution.

With the aspiration of better innovation for better tomorrow, wish you all the best and lead a happy productive life.
